Air Fryer Sweet Potato Fries

Description

Up your fry game by making delicious air fryer sweet potato fries at home. Seriously, there's no need to go to a restaurant to get crispy, sweet, and savory sweet potato fries when you have this easy 20-minute recipe.

You'll be making these sweet potato fries to go with every meal, so you better save it now.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground sweet paprika
